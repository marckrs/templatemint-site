TemplateMint — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

TemplateMint ("we", "us", "our") creates and sells digital Notion templates through Gumroad (minttemplate.gumroad.com). This policy explains what data we handle and how.

What we collect

We do not run our own accounts system or servers that store your data. When you buy a template, Gumroad processes the payment and shares your email address with us for the sole purpose of delivering the product and providing support. We do not collect, store, or process any other personal data.

How we use your data

  • To deliver purchased templates and provide customer support.
  • We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing.

Pinterest integration

Our Pinterest application publishes our own marketing content (pins) to our own TemplateMint business account. It does not access, collect, or store any data from Pinterest users or third parties.

Data retention & your rights (LGPD / GDPR)

We keep purchase-related email data only as long as needed for support and legal obligations. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

Cookies & tracking

We do not operate a website that sets tracking cookies. Purchases happen on Gumroad, which has its own privacy and cookie policies.
